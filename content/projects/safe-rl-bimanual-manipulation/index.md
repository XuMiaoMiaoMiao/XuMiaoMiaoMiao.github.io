This ongoing work studies safe reinforcement learning for dynamic bi-manual manipulation with two robotic arms operating in a shared workspace.

The project focuses on building a digital twin of a dual KUKA iiwa setup, defining manipulation tasks and safety constraints, and evaluating Lagrangian SAC for constrained policy learning.

The broader goal is to develop policies that can coordinate fast bi-manual motions while respecting task and safety constraints during training and deployment.
