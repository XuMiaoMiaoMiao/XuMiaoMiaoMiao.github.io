Developed a multiple non-inertial frames based air-ground cooperative framework for UAV transfer between UGVs.
The framework models UAV motion relative to moving ground vehicles, reducing the need for high-frequency global trajectory replanning and GPS-based world-frame dependence.

Key components:
- Differential modelling in UGV body frames.
- Stage-aware transition planning.
- Phase-adaptive model predictive control.
- Position reference design for cross-vehicle transfer.
