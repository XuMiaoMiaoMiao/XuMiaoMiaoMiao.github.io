Research Interests

My research focuses on reliable autonomy for aerial and cooperative robotic systems, including model predictive control, learning-based control, computer vision, and UAV/UGV cooperation.

  • Robotics and Autonomous Systems: reliable autonomy for aerial robots and heterogeneous robot teams.
  • Learning-based Control: data-driven modules integrated with interpretable model-based controllers.
  • Model Predictive Control: robust tracking, landing, and transition control for quadrotors.
  • UAV/UGV Cooperation: air-ground transfer tasks without relying on global state estimation.
  • Computer Vision: relative visual feedback for landing, cooperation, and robot perception.
